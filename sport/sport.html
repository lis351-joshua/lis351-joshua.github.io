I love playing basketball. I got into it when I was 9 years old. I would go out to the park and try to shoot some hoops. Eventually, the more I played, and the more I watched NBA basketball, I would learn the proper mechanics behind the shooting form, and I would eventually get better. I also used to play a lot of pickup basketball.

I did, however, get into the NBA before I played. I used to watch the Golden State Warriors (although, when I was a kid, I also had a fascination for the Grit n' Grind Grizzlies team in 2012-2013), and have been a fan of them to this day.
